# Variant Interpretation SOP

When interpreting variants:

1. Confirm gene symbol and transcript reference.
2. Gather literature and database evidence via NCBI/UniProt tools.
3. Assess splice impact when intronic or canonical splice site variants are involved.
4. Summarize evidence with retrieved_at timestamps; do not invent pathogenicity calls.
